Requirements

Application Procedure

Persons should apply for an appointment five days before their desired date. Registrants will be required to provide the following details: Age, Date of birth, Allergies, Area of work, Address, Telephone number, Email address. It is advised that persons take along their vaccination cards, a government-issued identification (ID) or letter from a Justice of the Peace (JP) to the sites to get receive vaccines.

Locations

All Vaccination Site locations open at 8:00pm and close at 5:00pm. On site, doses of the Pfizer, Astra-Zeneca, Johnson & Johnson and Moderna Vaccines will be available.
